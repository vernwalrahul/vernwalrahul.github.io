Hi! I am Rahul Vernwal. I am a 4th year undergrad at Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur. I am interested in motion planning and vision for robots and their intersection with machine learning. My work has been deployed/tested on prime air robots, UR10 and manipulators.

As a part of Kharagpur RoboSoccer Students' Group I work to build autonomous soccer playing robots. In particular, I work on path planning, controls and vision. I am working under Prof. Jayanta Mukhopadhyay for my undergraduate thesis.
